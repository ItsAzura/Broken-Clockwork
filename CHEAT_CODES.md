# Cheat Codes for Broken Clockwork (Dev Testing)

This document lists the available cheat codes implemented for rapid testing and debugging.

## Level Skip
- **Key**: `L`
- **Effect**: Instantly triggers the level clear sequence.
- **Usage**: Press during gameplay (while in `PLAYING` state) to skip to the next level or return to the title screen if it's the final level.

## Controls Refresher
- **Movement**: `Arrow Keys` or `WASD`
- **Jump**: `Space` or `Up`
- **Wind up objects**: `E` (Hold)
- **Pause**: `P` or `Esc`
- **Retry**: `R` (only on title/death screens)
